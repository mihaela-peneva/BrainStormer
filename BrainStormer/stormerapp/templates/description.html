Project Description

EEG data can be complex and challenging for lay users to comprehend. Many individuals cannot access solutions for streaming and interpreting their EEG data outside of lab environments. Our project aims to bridge this gap by providing a user-friendly online application that allows lay users to visualise and analyse their EEG data. Additionally, we detail a comprehensive review of relevant research in the field, which helps readers gain a better grasp of the concepts underlying the project.
